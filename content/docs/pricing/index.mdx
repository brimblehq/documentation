## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Got questions about the Free Plan? We’ve got answers:

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Is the Free Plan really free?" id="free-plan-cost">
    Yes! The Free Plan is **100% free** with no hidden fees or credit card required. You can use it indefinitely for personal projects.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed the Free Plan limits?" id="exceed-limits">
    If you exceed the Free Plan limits (e.g., bandwidth, build minutes), you’ll need to upgrade to the [Pro Plan](/pricing/plans/pro) or [Team Plan](/pricing/plans/team) to continue using Brimble.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use the Free Plan for commercial projects?" id="commercial-use">
    The Free Plan is designed for **personal and non-commercial use**. If you’re working on a commercial project, we recommend upgrading to the [Pro Plan](/pricing/plans/pro) for additional features and higher limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade from the Free Plan?" id="upgrade-free-plan">
    Upgrading is easy! Simply navigate to your [Billing Settings](/settings/billing) and choose the plan that best suits your needs. Your billing will be prorated based on your usage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use the Free Plan for open-source projects?" id="open-source">
    Absolutely! The Free Plan is a great choice for deploying and sharing open-source projects. If your project grows, you can always upgrade to a paid plan for more resources.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
