# Overview

At Brimble, we believe in **empowering developers** with tools that are not only powerful but also **affordable and transparent**. Our pricing is designed to grow with you, whether you're a solo developer working on a passion project or a large team building mission-critical applications. With **usage-based pricing**, you only pay for what you use, and our **developer-first approach** ensures you get the most value out of every dollar.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Got questions? We’ve got answers. Here are some of the most common questions about Brimble’s pricing:

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="What’s included in the Free Plan?" id="free-plan">
    The Free Plan is perfect for personal projects and getting started. It includes:
    - Personal Git Deployment
    - Live Project Preview
    - CLI Deployment
    - 10 Project Limit
    - Automatic HTTPS / SSL
    - Instant Deployment Rollbacks
    - External Integration Support
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the Pro Plan differ from the Free Plan?" id="pro-plan">
    The Pro Plan is designed for professional developers and small teams. In addition to all Free Plan features, it includes:
    - PR Preview Comments
    - 100 Project Limit
    - Brimble Web Analytics
    - Organization Git Deployment
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What makes the Team Plan unique?" id="team-plan">
    The Team Plan is built for teams and organizations with advanced needs. It includes all Pro Plan features, plus:
    - Scheduled Deployments
    - Multiple Concurrent Builds
    - Passworded Sites
    - 500 Project Limit
    - PR Previews
    - Premium Customer Support with Slack
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans later?" id="switch-plans">
    Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Your billing will be prorated based on your usage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my usage limits?" id="usage-limits">
    If you exceed your usage limits (e.g., bandwidth, build minutes), you’ll be charged based on our overage pricing. For example:
    - **Bandwidth**: $25 for an additional 100GB.
    - **Build Minutes**: $5 for an additional 1,000 minutes.
    - **Projects**: $2 for each additional project slot.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer custom plans for large teams?" id="custom-plans">
    Yes! If you have more than 7 team members or need custom configurations, [contact our sales team](/contact) for a tailored solution.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
