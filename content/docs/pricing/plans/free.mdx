## Free Plan Limitations

While the Free Plan is powerful, it’s designed for **small-scale projects**. Here are the key limitations to keep in mind:

- **10 Project Limit**: You can host up to 10 projects at a time. Need more? Upgrade to the Pro Plan for 100 projects or the Team Plan for 500 projects.
- **300 Build Minutes**: Free Plan users get 300 build minutes per month. Exceeded? Additional minutes cost $5 per 1,000 minutes.
- **30GB Bandwidth**: Free Plan includes 30GB of bandwidth per month. Need more? Additional bandwidth costs $25 per 100GB.
- **3-Day Log Retention**: Logs are retained for 3 days. Upgrade to Pro for 30 days or Team for 90 days.

<Callout type="warning">
  **Heads Up**: If your project grows beyond the Free Plan limits, you’ll be notified and can upgrade seamlessly to avoid interruptions.
</Callout>
