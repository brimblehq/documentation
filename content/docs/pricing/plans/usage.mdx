## Key Usage Metrics Explained

Here’s a detailed look at the **key usage metrics** and how they impact your pricing:

### 1. **Bandwidth**
Bandwidth refers to the amount of data transferred between your site and its visitors. Each plan includes a monthly bandwidth allowance:
- **Free Plan**: 30GB/month
- **Pro Plan**: 150GB/month
- **Team Plan**: 500GB/month

<Callout type="warning">
  **Overage Pricing**: If you exceed your bandwidth limit, additional bandwidth costs **$25 for every 100GB**.
</Callout>

### 2. **Build Minutes**
Build minutes represent the time spent building your projects. Each plan includes a monthly build minute allowance:
- **Free Plan**: 300 minutes/month
- **Pro Plan**: 10K minutes/month
- **Team Plan**: 25K minutes/month

<Callout type="warning">
  **Overage Pricing**: If you exceed your build minute limit, additional minutes cost **$5 for every 1,000 minutes**.
</Callout>

### 3. **Projects**
Projects refer to the number of individual sites or applications you can host. Each plan includes a project slot limit:
- **Free Plan**: 10 slots
- **Pro Plan**: 100 slots
- **Team Plan**: 500 slots

<Callout type="warning">
  **Overage Pricing**: If you exceed your project slot limit, additional slots cost **$2 per project/month**.
</Callout>

### 4. **Log Retention**
Log retention determines how long your deployment logs are stored. Each plan includes a log retention period:
- **Free Plan**: 3 days
- **Pro Plan**: 30 days
- **Team Plan**: 90 days

<Callout type="tip">
  **Pro Tip**: Longer log retention helps with debugging and monitoring, especially for larger teams.
</Callout>

### 5. **Analytics**
Analytics provides insights into your site’s performance. Each plan includes analytics for a limited number of sites:
- **Free Plan**: Not included
- **Pro Plan**: Enabled for 50 sites
- **Team Plan**: Enabled for 100 sites

<Callout type="warning">
  **Overage Pricing**: If you exceed your analytics limit, additional sites cost **$5 per site/month**.
</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Got questions about usage-based pricing? We’ve got answers.

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my usage limits?" id="exceed-limits">
    If you exceed your usage limits (e.g., bandwidth, build minutes), you’ll be charged based on our overage pricing. For example:
    - **Bandwidth**: $25 for an additional 100GB.
    - **Build Minutes**: $5 for an additional 1,000 minutes.
    - **Projects**: $2 for each additional project slot.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I monitor my usage in real-time?" id="monitor-usage">
    Yes, you can monitor your usage in real-time through the Brimble dashboard. We’ll also notify you if you’re approaching your limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade my plan to increase my limits?" id="upgrade-plan">
    Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher plan at any time to increase your usage limits. Your billing will be prorated based on your usage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a way to reduce my usage?" id="reduce-usage">
    Yes, you can optimize your usage by:
    - Reducing build times with efficient build configurations.
    - Compressing assets to reduce bandwidth usage.
    - Archiving unused projects to free up project slots.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer custom plans for high-usage teams?" id="custom-plans">
    Yes! If your team has high usage needs, [contact our sales team](/contact) for a custom plan tailored to your requirements.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
