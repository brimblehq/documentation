## Team Plan Limitations

While the Team Plan is designed for **large-scale projects**, it’s important to understand its limits:

- **500 Project Limit**: You can host up to 500 projects at a time. Need more? [Contact our sales team](/contact) for custom solutions.
- **25K Build Minutes**: Team Plan users get 25,000 build minutes per month. Exceeded? Additional minutes cost $5 per 1,000 minutes.
- **500GB Bandwidth**: Team Plan includes 500GB of bandwidth per month. Need more? Additional bandwidth costs $25 per 100GB.
- **90-Day Log Retention**: Logs are retained for 90 days. For longer retention, [contact our sales team](/contact).

<Callout type="warning">
  **Heads Up**: If your project grows beyond the Team Plan limits, you’ll be notified and can work with our sales team for a custom solution.
</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Got questions about the Team Plan? We’ve got answers.

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="How many team members can I add?" id="team-members">
    The Team Plan allows you to add 3, 5, or 7 team members by default. For larger teams, [contact our sales team](/contact) for custom pricing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed the Team Plan limits?" id="exceed-limits">
    If you exceed the Team Plan limits (e.g., bandwidth, build minutes), you’ll be charged based on our overage pricing. For example:
    - **Bandwidth**: $25 for an additional 100GB.
    - **Build Minutes**: $5 for an additional 1,000 minutes.
    - **Projects**: $2 for each additional project slot.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I downgrade from the Team Plan later?" id="downgrade-later">
    Yes, you can downgrade to the Pro or Free Plan at any time. Your billing will be adjusted accordingly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does the Team Plan include custom domains?" id="custom-domains">
    Yes, the Team Plan supports custom domains with automatic HTTPS / SSL.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I try the Team Plan before committing?" id="try-team">
    Absolutely! You can start with the Free or Pro Plan and upgrade to Team at any time. Your billing will be prorated based on your usage.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
