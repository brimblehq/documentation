## Pro Plan Limitations

While the Pro Plan is designed for **medium-sized projects**, it’s important to understand its limits:

- **100 Project Limit**: You can host up to 100 projects at a time. Need more? Upgrade to the Team Plan for 500 projects.
- **10K Build Minutes**: Pro Plan users get 10,000 build minutes per month. Exceeded? Additional minutes cost $5 per 1,000 minutes.
- **150GB Bandwidth**: Pro Plan includes 150GB of bandwidth per month. Need more? Additional bandwidth costs $25 per 100GB.
- **30-Day Log Retention**: Logs are retained for 30 days. Upgrade to the Team Plan for 90 days.

<Callout type="warning">
  **Heads Up**: If your project grows beyond the Pro Plan limits, you’ll be notified and can upgrade seamlessly to avoid interruptions.
</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Got questions about the Pro Plan? We’ve got answers.

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Can I use the Pro Plan for team collaboration?" id="team-collab">
    Yes, the Pro Plan supports organization Git deployment, making it ideal for small teams. However, for advanced team features like Slack support and team member management, consider the [Team Plan](/pricing/plans/team).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed the Pro Plan limits?" id="exceed-limits">
    If you exceed the Pro Plan limits (e.g., bandwidth, build minutes), you’ll be charged based on our overage pricing. For example:
    - **Bandwidth**: $25 for an additional 100GB.
    - **Build Minutes**: $5 for an additional 1,000 minutes.
    - **Projects**: $2 for each additional project slot.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I downgrade from the Pro Plan later?" id="downgrade-later">
    Yes, you can downgrade to the Free Plan at any time. Your billing will be adjusted accordingly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does the Pro Plan include custom domains?" id="custom-domains">
    Yes, the Pro Plan supports custom domains with automatic HTTPS / SSL.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I try the Pro Plan before committing?" id="try-pro">
    Absolutely! You can start with the Free Plan and upgrade to Pro at any time. Your billing will be prorated based on your usage.
  </Accordion>
