## For Freelancers and Small Teams

If you’re a **freelancer** delivering client projects or a **small team** building a startup, the **Pro Plan** gives you the tools to **deploy faster and scale effortlessly**.

### Why Choose the Pro Plan?
- **Advanced Features**: PR previews, organization Git deployment, and Brimble Web Analytics.
- **Scalability**: Host up to 100 projects and enjoy 10K build minutes per month.
- **Reliability**: Premium features like instant rollbacks and automatic HTTPS.
