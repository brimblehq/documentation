## Key Add-Ons Explained

Here’s a detailed look at the **key add-ons** and how they can benefit your workflow:

### 1. **Team Members**
Collaborate seamlessly with your team by adding more members to your plan. Pricing is based on the number of members:
- **3 Members**: $30/month
- **5 Members**: $50/month
- **7 Members**: $70/month
- **Custom Teams**: Contact sales for teams larger than 7 members.

<Callout type="tip">
  **Pro Tip**: Adding team members is ideal for growing teams that need shared access to projects and deployments.
</Callout>

### 2. **Concurrent Builds**
Speed up your development process by running multiple builds simultaneously. Pricing is based on the number of concurrent builds:
- **1 Build**: $15/month
- **2 Builds**: $30/month
- **3 Builds**: $45/month
- **4 Builds**: $60/month
- **5 Builds**: $75/month
- **6 Builds**: $90/month
- **7 Builds**: $105/month

<Callout type="tip">
  **Pro Tip**: Concurrent builds are perfect for teams working on multiple projects or large-scale applications.
</Callout>

### 3. **Internal Slack Support**
Get **premium support** directly in your Slack workspace for faster issue resolution and personalized assistance.
- **Slack Support**: $15/month

<Callout type="tip">
  **Pro Tip**: Slack support is ideal for teams that need real-time assistance and faster response times.
</Callout>

### 4. **Grafana Dashboard**
Access **advanced monitoring and analytics** with Grafana, included in the Pro and Team Plans. Visualize your logs, metrics, and traces in real-time.

<Callout type="tip">
  **Pro Tip**: Grafana dashboards are perfect for teams that need deep insights into their deployments and performance.
</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Got questions about add-ons? We’ve got answers.

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Can I add team members to the Free Plan?" id="team-members-free">
    No, team members are only available for the Pro and Team Plans. Consider upgrading to add team members.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I add concurrent builds to my plan?" id="add-concurrent-builds">
    You can add concurrent builds directly from your Brimble dashboard. Simply navigate to the “Add-Ons” section and select the number of builds you need.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Slack support available for all plans?" id="slack-support-plans">
    Yes, Slack support is available as an add-on for all plans. It costs **$15/month**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I remove an add-on later?" id="remove-add-ons">
    Yes, you can remove add-ons at any time. Your billing will be adjusted accordingly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer custom add-ons for large teams?" id="custom-add-ons">
