---
title: 'Import an Existing Project'
description: 'Seamlessly create a new project on Brimble by importing your existing frontend or full-stack project, built on any of our supported frameworks.'
---

Leverage the power of **Brimble** to bring your existing projects to life with seamless integration and streamlined deployment.  
This guide walks you through the process of importing your project, configuring settings, and fully utilizing Brimble's advanced features to enhance your workflow.

import { Step, Steps } from 'fumadocs-ui/components/steps';

<Steps>

<Step>

### Connect to Your Git Provider

To start, navigate to the **New Project** page and select the **Import Git Repository** option. Choose the Git provider you wish to connect with, such as GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket.

Follow the prompts to sign in and authorize Brimble to access your repositories. This ensures Brimble can seamlessly fetch your project and handle deployments.

By connecting your Git provider, Brimble enables continuous deployment, ensuring every change you push to the repository triggers an automatic deployment.

<img src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1716221839/Screenshot_73_jqkd07.png" width="YOUR_WIDTH_VALUE" alt="connecting your Git provider" />

</Step>

<Step>

### Import Your Repository

Once connected, you’ll see a list of your repositories. Locate the repository you want to import and click **Import**.

This action transfers your project into Brimble’s system, enabling powerful deployment and management features tailored to your needs.

Brimble optimizes your build configurations, detects framework settings, and streamlines your deployment pipeline.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1716222419/Screenshot_75_lznwza.png)

</Step>

<Step>

### Configure Project Settings (Optional)

After importing, Brimble automatically detects your framework and build settings. However, you can fine-tune these configurations for greater control:

- **Project Branch**: Select the branch you want to deploy.
- **Deployment Directory**: Specify which directory to deploy.
- **Build Settings**: Adjust build commands, install commands, or development commands.
- **Environment Variables**: Define variables for managing different environments.

Expand the relevant sections in the **Basic Build Settings** and **Environment Variables** tabs to customize your project as needed.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1716224393/Screenshot_77_jgo5oy.png)

</Step>

<Step>

### Deploy Your Project

Once your configurations are ready, press the **Deploy** button. Brimble will initiate the deployment process, leveraging your selected settings to create and deploy the project.

Watch as your application is transformed into a live deployment, ready to scale and perform seamlessly.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1716297101/Screenshot_81_wywgao.png)

</Step>

<Step>

### Enjoy the Confetti!

Congratulations! Your project is now live. To view the deployment, go to your Brimble dashboard, select the project, and access the auto-generated domain.

Share the URL with your team or users and showcase your live application instantly.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1716298155/Screenshot_86_oba8gu.png)

</Step>

</Steps>
