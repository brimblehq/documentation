## Getting Started with Brimble

Welcome to Brimble, your ultimate platform for effortlessly deploying **JAMSTACK** frontend apps!
In this step-by-step guide, we'll walk you through the process of getting started with Brimble, from creating an account to deploying your first project.

## Before You Begin

To begin your journey with Brimble, you'll need to create an account.
If you haven't already, head over to our website and sign up for a new Brimble account. Choose the plan that suits your needs, and you're ready to go!

## Customizing Your Experience

Brimble supports a wide range of frontend frameworks, making it a versatile choice for developers.
Whether you're using React, Nuxt, Astro, Next.js, Vue.js, or any other framework, Brimble has you covered. 
Throughout this guide, we'll provide specific instructions tailored to your chosen framework.
