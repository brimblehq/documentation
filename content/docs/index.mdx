## Why Brimble?

### **1. Built for Developers, by Developers**
We know how frustrating it can be to deal with bloated platforms and hidden fees. That’s why we built Brimble—to give you a **simple, transparent, and powerful** deployment experience. From solo developers to large teams, Brimble adapts to your needs.

### **2. Full-Stack Flexibility**
Brimble supports **frontend, backend, and full-stack applications**, including:
- **JavaScript Frameworks**: React, Next.js, Vue, Angular, and more.
- **Backend Runtimes**: Node.js, PHP/Laravel, and Python (coming soon).
- **Static Sites**: HTML, CSS, and Markdown-based sites.

No matter what you’re building, Brimble has the tools to make deployment seamless.

### **3. Scalable and Reliable**
From **side projects** to **enterprise applications**, Brimble scales with you. Features like **automatic HTTPS**, **instant rollbacks**, and **usage-based pricing** ensure your apps are fast, secure, and cost-effective.

### **4. Developer-First Features**
- **PR Previews**: Collaborate with your team by previewing changes in real-time.
- **AI-Assisted DevOps**: Debug and optimize your deployments with built-in AI tools.
- **Seamless Integrations**: Connect your GitHub or GitLab repo and deploy in seconds.
