title: 'Buy a Domain'
description: 'Secure your domain with Brimble. Expand your online reach and establish a memorable digital identity.'
---

Harness the power of **Brimble** to discover and secure a domain that perfectly aligns with your brand. A personalized domain enhances credibility and helps you connect with your audience more effectively.

import { Step, Steps } from 'fumadocs-ui/components/steps';

<Steps>

<Step>

### Find a Domain

Start by finding the perfect domain that represents your brand or identity. Visit Brimble's domain search tool at [beta.brimble.io/domains/new](https://beta.brimble.io/domains/new) and search for an available domain, such as "Vibecityng."

Prices vary based on the TLD (e.g., .com, .io), so explore different options to find the ideal match for your needs.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1737109817/Screenshot_180_b9ed2j.png)

</Step>

<Step>

### Choose Your Domain & Add to Cart

Once you’ve found the domain that fits your brand, select it and add it to your cart. Decide how long you want to register the domain for, whether it’s 1 year, 2 years, or up to 10 years.

This step ensures you’re securing the domain for the duration that best fits your plans.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1737109817/Screenshot_181_topl7x.png)

</Step>

<Step>

### Choose Your Payment Method and Confirm

It’s time to make your purchase official. Select your preferred payment method—Brimble supports credit/debit cards and cryptocurrency. Your payment information is securely stored for automatic renewal, ensuring you never lose access to your domain.

Review your order, confirm the payment, and secure your place on the web.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1737109817/Screenshot_182_bvvuoc.png)

</Step>

<Step>

### Assign Your Domain to a Project

From your Brimble dashboard, navigate to the project you’d like to associate with your new domain. In the project settings, click on **"Add a custom domain"** in the top-right corner.

Enter the domain you’ve just purchased to link it to your project. With this step completed, your new domain is live and connected to your application.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1716305302/Screenshot_96_xh38tm.png)

</Step>

</Steps>
